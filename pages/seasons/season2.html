Season 2

This season introduces Martian Gunnery Sergeant Bobbie Draper as her squad encounters a Protomolecule hybrid on the agricultural colony on the planet of Ganymede. The crew of the Rocinante discovers the Protomolecule has taken over Eros and is threatening the safety of the entire solar system. From there, the larger conspiracy to control the protomolecule is further unraveled, as the crew of the Rocinante, UN Undersecretary Avasarala and Martian solider Bobbie Draper attempt to prevent system wide war AND stop a mysterious new creature.

Season 2 premiered with a double episode on February 1, 2017 and consists of 13 episodes total.
