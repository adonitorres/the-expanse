Season 1

In Season 1, the disappearance of rich-girl-turned-political-activist Julie Mao linked the lives of Ceres' detective Joe Miller (Thomas Jane), accidental ship captain James Holden (Steven Strait) and U.N. politician Chrisjen Avasarala (Shohreh Aghdashloo).

Despite their many differences, the thrown-together crew forms a bond that can only be compared to that of a family. Amidst political tension between Earth, Mars and the Asteroid Belt, these individuals unravel the single greatest conspiracy of all time.

Season 1 premiered online on November 23, 2015 and on SyFy and Space on December 14, 2015, and consists of 10 episodes of one hour each.
