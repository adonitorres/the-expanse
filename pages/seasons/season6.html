Season 6

Season 6 is rumored to follow the events of the book "Babylon's Ashes" however, no other information has been provided to confirm or refute this rumor. In any case, the events of Season 5 left with the Free Navy assisting a rogue fleet of the MCRN in entering one of the many Ring Gates while the rest of the solar system deals with the aftermath of Marco Inaros's attacks on the UN and the MCR.

Season 6 is the sixth and final season of Amazon's The Expanse and will mark the end to the TV series. Amazon officially ordered it on November 24, 2020. The date for the premiere of Season 6 has yet to be announced.
