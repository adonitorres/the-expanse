BobBiE DRapEr

Gunnery Sergeant Roberta ("Bobbie") Draper is the toughest member of her squad and a disciplinarian when it comes to anyone acting out of line. She is a fiercely nationalistic Martian dedicated to the dream of terraforming the planet. Her family has worked hard to make the Martian dream come true...in the next hundred years. Despite knowing she will never see a Mars with lush vegetation and flowing water, Bobbie Draper has a never quit attitude which makes her the ideal soldier.

Bobbie Draper is played by the Somoan New Zealand actress Frankie Adams.
