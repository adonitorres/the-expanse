Seasons

The Expanse is an American science fiction television series developed by Mark Fergus and Hawk Ostby, based on the series of novels of the same name by James S. A. Corey. The series is set in a future where humanity has colonized the solar system and it follows a disparate band of protagonists as they unwittingly unravel and place themselves at the center of a conspiracy that threatens the system's fragile state of cold war.

The Expanse (TV series) was originally adapted from the books by the SyFy channel where it ran for three seasons before SyFy dropped it in May 2018. An overwhelming number of fans protested the cancellation, petitioning for its renewel by Amazon Studies and Netflix. Amazon Studies picked up the show for a fourth season, eventually renewing it for a fifth and sixth season.
