Factions

The Expanse universe is home to a wide variety of factions, the largest of which are The United Nations (UN), The Martian Congressional Republic (MCR), and The Outer Planets Alliance (OPA). During the course of the series, we learn of technology from an extremely advanced alien civilization. Who was this advanced civilization and what caused their disappearance is an ongoing question as humankind struggles to deal with its own problems between the factions.
