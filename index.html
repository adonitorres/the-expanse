The Expanse is a television series adapted from the novels of James S. A. Corey, the joint pen name of Daniel Abraham and Ty Franck.

Hundreds of years in the future, humans have colonized the solar system. The U.N. controls
Earth. Mars is an independent military power. The planets rely on the resources of the
Asteroid Belt, where air and water are more precious than gold. For decades,
tensions have been rising between these three places. Earth, Mars and the Belt
are now on the brink of war. And all it will take is a single spark.
